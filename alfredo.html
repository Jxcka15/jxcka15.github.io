Alfredo Sauce


Ingredients


  • 25g butter
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed or finely grated
  • 300ml double cream
  • 1tsp Italian dried herbs
  • 100g parmesan, grated
  • Â½ small bunch parsley, finely chopped
  • 400g pasta, cooked

Step 1


Melt the butter in a pan over a medium-low heat and fry the garlic for 1 min until softened but not browned.
Stir in the cream and mixed herbs, and bring the cream to a gentle simmer, lowering the temperature if necessary. Add the parmesan,
stirring continuously until it has melted. Sprinkle over the parsley (you can reserve a little to garnish, if you like).
Fold into the cooked pasta and serve.
